# Contact Page

Purpose:

Allow customers to contact the shop.

---

1. Header

Display:

Title: Contact Us
Short description: We're here to help with orders, delivery questions, and custom floral requests.

Spacing:

py-16

---

2. Contact Info

Display:

Address  
Phone  
Email  
Business hours

Layout:

Single column card in a stacked page flow.

---

3. Contact Form

Fields:

Name  
Email  
Message

Button:

Send Message

Form must:

Have spacing between fields
Remain responsive
Be readable

---

4. Auto-Fill From Product Order

When coming from a product detail page Order Now button:

Route format: /contact?product=<product-id>

Behavior:

Selected item label is shown in the form section
Message field is prefilled with product name and price

Example:

Hi, I would like to order Blush Garden Bouquet ($48). Please share delivery options.

Footer:

Footer stays at bottom through shared app layout.
